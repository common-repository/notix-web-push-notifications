* [In-app monetization](https://notix.co/blog/in-app-push-notifications-dos-donts-best-practices/): reach your mobile app users with vibrant push and interstitial promos and scale your retention efforts.

* [On-site monetization](https://notix.co/blog/push-notifications-explained/): send instant on-screen updates, news, and promotions to your website visitors and ensure maximum engagement.

* Parallel testing and database migration: if you already have a push provider but want to try NOTIX, [make your decision](https://notix.co/blog/how-to-choose-a-push-provider/) as objective as possible. * RSS Feed: make your RSS Feed more advanced and flexible - turn it into push notifications and send immediate messages to subscribers.

* [Triggered Push](https://notix.co/blog/sending-triggered-push-notifications-through-api-integration/): send action-based alerts that fit your users’ behavior to catch them when they are most ready to act.

* Updates and news categories subscription: if you have a large resource divided into themed sections, offer your subscribers to [choose those that interest them](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iDl8jJRVUqc) the most to bring them maximum value.

* Flexible integration: whether you're a developer or not, NOTIX offers versatile integration options, including an API, WP plugin, and SAAS.

* Database flexibility: we cater to businesses of all sizes, offering seamless database management for both individual and corporate clients.

* GEO-targeting: target your audience effectively with location-based notifications. Perfect for promoting nearby events, stores, and more. City-level targeting is also available.

* Device targeting: reach your audience [on both desktop and mobile devices](https://notix.co/blog/notix-push-notifications-across-various-devices-on-ios-and-android/), always with user consent. It's a powerful way to boost engagement.

* [Segmentation & automation](https://notix.co/blog/audience-segmentation-strategies-for-push-notifications/): customize your push notifications with criteria like language, region, subscription period, platform, and more. Automate messages to save time.

* Real-time statistics: [analyze the success](https://notix.co/blog/measuring-your-success-push-notifications-metrics-and-how-to-make-sense-of-them/) of your push notifications with ease. Identify high-read and high-click-through notifications to fine-tune your messaging.

== Installation ==

1. Install Notix from the WordPress.org plugin directory or by uploading the Notix plugin folder to your wp-content/plugins directory.
2. Activate the Notix plugin from your WordPress settings dashboard.
3. Open Notix plugin settings on left side menu.
4. Follow the instructions on the plugin page.
